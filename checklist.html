IRB Decision Guide

IRB Criteria for Approval

Use this checklist to ensure compliance with IRB criteria before submitting your research proposal:

General Considerations

  • Adequate expertise has been consulted for the study.
  • All materials are complete and accurate.

Criteria for Approval of Research

  • Risks to subjects are minimized through proper research design.
  • Risks are reasonable in relation to potential benefits.
  • Subject selection is equitable and appropriate.
  • The research plan includes monitoring for subject safety.
  • There are adequate protections for subject privacy and data confidentiality.
  • The informed consent process is clearly documented and appropriate.
  • There are additional safeguards for vulnerable populations.

Consent Process

  • Legally effective informed consent will be obtained from subjects.
  • The consent process provides ample time for subjects to consider participation.
  • Consent documents are presented in an understandable language.
  • Consent includes all necessary disclosures as outlined in the criteria checklist.
